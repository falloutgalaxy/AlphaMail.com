Our Fleet

Professional Operations Every Day

Fleet Operations

Alpha Mail's fleet is the backbone of our organisation. Every vehicle is maintained to professional standards to ensure reliable, safe and efficient operations throughout every delivery.

Fleet Standards

Vehicle Inspections

Vehicles are checked regularly to ensure operational readiness.

Professional Appearance

Vehicles are expected to remain clean and presentable.

Damage Reporting

Issues must be identified and resolved promptly.

Equipment Checks

Operational equipment is checked before use.

Safety Priorities

1. Vehicle Control

Maintain full control of the vehicle at all times.

2. Public Safety

Protect pedestrians, road users and surrounding environments.

3. Delivery Completion

Deliveries should only be completed safely.

Fleet Maintenance

Bodywork

Vehicles must remain free from significant visible damage.

Lighting

Headlights and tail lights must remain operational.

Roadworthiness

Unsafe vehicles must never be used.

Reliability

Every vehicle must meet Alpha Mail standards before deployment.

Our Commitment

Alpha Mail is committed to preserving and maintaining a professional fleet that supports reliable operations every day.


If a delivery cannot be completed safely, it should not be completed at all.
